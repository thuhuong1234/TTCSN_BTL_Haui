Design & Build a multisite for a leading food importer & distributor

Food Source International (FSI) is the leading food import, export, marketing and distribution company dedicated to sourcing and selling premium quality food products for the hospitality industry in Vietnam, the UAE and throughout the region.

FSI wanted to build two websites with similar design and structure, but with completely different content for 2 markets. We consulted them to instead build a multisite, which allows the company to manage multiple websites on a centralized dashboard.

About Us

The About Us section contains a page to introduce the company, its unique selling points, facilities and team. The Customers page listed out their customer segments so that the potential customers can easily find information that is relevant to their industries when visiting the website.

Products

One of the biggest challenges of this project is to present the brands in each product category in an interesting layout that also allows visitor to access the details with just a couple of clicks.

THE OUTCOME

  • Professional, user-centered website design with better user experience and content strategy
  • Custom WordPress CMS that allows easy content update & maintenance on both websites
  • Healthy organic SEO growth with Health Score of 97/100 (ahrefs), Google PageSpeed 91/100 (Desktop), 73/100 (Mobile) with top ranking for important keywords.
